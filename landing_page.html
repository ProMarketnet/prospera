Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from generic market research?

Prospera learns your specific business profile and only finds opportunities that fit your products, target markets, and growth goals. Instead of generic industry reports, you get personalized intelligence with actionable next steps.

How quickly will I see results?

Most customers identify their first qualified leads within 7 days. Your first intelligence report is generated within 24 hours of setup. Revenue impact typically begins within 30-60 days as you act on opportunities.

What if Prospera doesn't find good opportunities?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If Prospera doesn't identify at least 10 qualified leads and 3 actionable opportunities in your first month, we'll refund your payment completely.

Is my business data secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and never share your data with third parties. Your competitive intelligence stays completely confidential to your business.

Can I customize what Prospera focuses on?

Yes! You can adjust your focus regions, target markets, competitor tracking, and growth priorities anytime. Prospera adapts its intelligence gathering to match your evolving business needs.

What industries does Prospera work with?

We specialize in SME manufacturers across fashion & apparel, consumer goods, specialty manufacturing, electronics, and home & lifestyle products. If you make physical products, Prospera can help you grow.
